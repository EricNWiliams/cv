The Skateboarding Geologist

I am a recent graduate from The University of Texas at Dallas with a B.S. in Geosciences. I am aslo an aspiring web developer. I started studying Geology at UTD in 2015 and will be graduating with a B.S. in the summer of 2020. Prior to that I spent nearly ten years working in various industries from 2006 – 2015. And prior to that I graduated from high school in 2006.

I decided to start studying geology after spending five years working in the debt collection industry.I enjoyed my work at first, but after a while the appeal began to wear thin. I was bored from working at a desk all day and sick of collecting debts. However, my experience with various debts, particularly student loan debts, helped me realize I could afford to go to school and not work a full-time job.

With this knowledge I quit my job, took out student loans and enrolled at UTD. I knew I wanted to study a science. I chose one that was as different from my previous work as I could find, geology. For the last four years I have traveled from mountains to deserts studying earth and rock science. Now that I am nearing graduation, I don’t know what I am going to do next, but it sure has been a lot of fun.
